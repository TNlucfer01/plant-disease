# Tamil Nadu Crop Diseases and Kaggle Datasets

**Document Overview**  
This document compiles common diseases affecting major crops cultivated in Tamil Nadu and provides curated links to publicly available Kaggle image datasets suitable for machine learning-based disease detection. The datasets primarily consist of RGB images of leaves or plant parts, annotated by disease class, and often include healthy samples.

Data is organized by crop category for easy reference. Most datasets require a free Kaggle account to download. Always verify the latest version and citation requirements on the respective Kaggle page.

**General Multi-Crop Dataset (Highly Recommended)**  
- **New Plant Diseases Dataset** (≈87,000 RGB images across 38 classes, including tomato, maize, pepper/chilli, grape, potato, and others)  
  [Download Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vipoooool/new-plant-diseases-dataset)

## 1. Food Crops

### Rice (Paddy)
Common diseases covered in datasets: Bacterial Leaf Blight, Brown Spot, Leaf Blast, Sheath Blight, Leaf Scald, Tungro, etc.

- [Rice Disease Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/anshulm257/rice-disease-dataset) – 3,829 images (Bacterial Leaf Blight, Brown Spot, Healthy, Leaf Blast, Leaf Scald, Sheath Blight)
- [Rice Leaf Diseases Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vbookshelf/rice-leaf-diseases)
- [Rice Diseases Image Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/minhhuy2810/rice-diseases-image-dataset)
- [Rice disease dataset DMD_preprocessed](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sudheshkm/rice-disease-dataset-dmd-preprocessed)
- [Paddy Doctor: Paddy Disease Classification](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/paddy-disease-classification) (Competition dataset with 10 classes)

### Maize (Corn)
Common diseases: Common Rust, Gray Leaf Spot, Blight, Healthy.

- [Corn or Maize Leaf Disease Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/smaranjitghose/corn-or-maize-leaf-disease-dataset) – Built from PlantVillage and PlantDoc

### Sorghum (Cholam) and Millets
- [Sorghum Disease Image Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rogfsdafdsafdsa/sorghum) – Includes Anthracnose, Grain Molds, Smuts, etc.

## 2. Plantation Crops

### Rubber
- [Rubber Disease Final Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kongkityeesai/rubber-disease-final-datasetss) – Leaf diseases including Anthracnose, Black Spot, Leaf Blight, Powdery Mildew

### Tea
Common diseases: Red Leaf Spot, Algal Leaf Spot, Bird’s Eye Spot, Gray Blight, White Spot, Anthracnose, Brown Blight.

- [Identifying Disease in Tea Leaves](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shashwatwork/identifying-disease-in-tea-leafs)
- [teaLeafBD: Tea Leaf Disease Detection](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bmshahriaalam/tealeafbd-tea-leaf-disease-detection)

### Coffee
- Coffee Leaf Diseases datasets are available on Kaggle (search for "coffee leaf diseases" for rust, miner, etc.).

### Coconut
- Coconut Disease and Pest Infestation Dataset (search for "coconut diseases" on Kaggle)

### Cashew
Limited dedicated datasets. Use general multi-crop resources.

## 3. Horticulture (Fruits & Vegetables)

### Mango
Common diseases: Anthracnose, Bacterial Canker, Die Back, Powdery Mildew, etc.

- [Mango Leaf Disease Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aryashah2k/mango-leaf-disease-dataset) – 7 diseases (Anthracnose, Bacterial Canker, Cutting Weevil, Die Back, Gall Midge, Powdery Mildew, Sooty Mould)

### Banana
- [Banana Leaf Spot Diseases (BananaLSD) Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shifatearman/bananalsd) – Sigatoka, Cordana, Pestalotiopsis, Healthy

### Cassava (Tapioca)
- Cassava Leaf Disease Classification (search for "cassava leaf disease" on Kaggle)

### Tomato
Strong coverage in the New Plant Diseases Dataset (Early Blight, Late Blight, Bacterial Spot, Leaf Mold, Mosaic Virus, etc.).

### Brinjal (Eggplant)
- Eggplant Disease Recognition Dataset (search for "eggplant disease" on Kaggle)

### Bhendi (Okra)
- Yellow Vein Mosaic Disease datasets available (search for "okra yellow vein" or "ladyfinger disease")

### Onion
- Onion Diseases Dataset (search for "onion diseases" on Kaggle)

## 4. Commercial / Cash Crops

### Sugarcane
Common diseases: Red Rot, Mosaic, Rust, Yellow, Bacterial Blight, Smut.

- [Sugarcane Plant Diseases Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/akilesh253/sugarcane-plant-diseases-dataset) – 19,926 images (Bacterial Blight, Healthy, Mosaic, Red Rot, Rust, Yellow)
- [Sugarcane Leaf Disease Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nirmalsankalana/sugarcane-leaf-disease-dataset)

### Cotton
- Cotton Plant Disease Dataset (search for "cotton plant disease" on Kaggle)

### Groundnut
- Groundnut Plant Leaf Data (search for "groundnut leaf disease" on Kaggle)

## 5. Spices
- **Chillies / Pepper**: Covered in New Plant Diseases Dataset and dedicated chilli disease datasets (search for "chilli plant diseases")

Limited dedicated datasets for Turmeric, Tamarind, and Coriander. Use general spice/leaf disease collections.

## 6. Flowers (Jasmine, Marigold, etc.)
No large dedicated Kaggle datasets identified. General ornamental plant disease datasets may be used.

## Additional Notes
- For crops with limited datasets, the **New Plant Diseases Dataset** offers transferable learning opportunities due to its broad coverage of similar symptoms.
- Datasets are periodically updated on Kaggle. Verify image count, class distribution, and license before use.
- **Recommended Citation Practice**: Credit the original dataset authors in any research or publication.
- For real-field images, consider PlantDoc alongside Kaggle resources.
